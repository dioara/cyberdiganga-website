## Services Section
**Title**: "Our Comprehensive Security Services"

### 1. Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) Services
- Develop and implement security policies, procedures, and frameworks aligned with ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR, and other standards
- Conduct risk assessments to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities
- Assist with certification processes, including ISO 27001 and GDPR readiness
- Develop robust data privacy programs and ensure compliance with data protection regulations

### 2. Cybersecurity Consulting & Advisory
- Provide strategic guidance on cybersecurity initiatives aligned with business objectives
- Design and implement effective security architectures tailored to client needs
- Conduct threat modelling and vulnerability management using industry-standard frameworks like STRIDE and OWASP

### 3. Incident Response & Threat Management
- Develop incident response plans and handle post-incident forensic investigations
- Monitor, detect, and respond to cybersecurity incidents promptly
- Implement advanced solutions such as SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response) to enhance incident handling

### 4. Cloud Security & Identity Management
- Manage cloud security solutions, such as Microsoft Defender and Purview
- Implement identity and access management (IAM) solutions for seamless and secure access to business resources

### 5. Security Awareness & Training
- Conduct phishing simulations and hands-on security awareness training for employees
- Foster a culture of security across the organisation through periodic workshops and programs

### 6. Managed Security Services
- Provide 24/7 threat monitoring and management using SIEM and SOC solutions like Microsoft Sentinel
- Regularly review and optimise security technologies to ensure maximum protection
- Perform vulnerability assessments and security audits
